We transform the future of Africa in tech

EduTech partners with more than 200 leading universities and companies to bring flexible, affordable, job-relevant online learning to individuals and organizations worldwide. We offer a range of learning opportunities —from hands-on projects and courses to job-ready certificates and degree programs.

Purpose

EduTech is a life-changing programme!

Its mission is to upskill and grow young, job-ready talent with intermediate and advanced in-demand tech skills to become Africa’s future leaders and entrepreneurs, transforming 60,000 lives – enabling 40,000 with foundational digital skills and 20,000 with professional advanced tech skills.

Programmes

There is no other programme like it on the continent, providing the same e-learning package of quality tech training from Udacity; in Cloud DevOps, FullStack, Data Analysis and Cloud Developer, coupled with career services and community through ALX and The ROOM, enabling graduates to get a job in tech companies all over the world. Through these fully sponsored programmes, young Africans gain the skills and the connection, at no cost, to grant them access to the tech ecosystem.
